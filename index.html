Cislunar Habitats

Designing sustainable living spaces for the Earth-Moon system — inflatable modules, surface outposts, orbital stations, and advanced habitats to support long-duration human presence in cislunar space, powering NASA's Artemis program and the lunar economy.

From expandable lunar base camps at the South Pole to Gateway-derived orbital habitats, cislunar habitats provide radiation protection, life support, resource utilization, and comfortable quarters for astronauts exploring, mining, and building the future beyond Earth.
